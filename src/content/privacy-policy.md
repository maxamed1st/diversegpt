# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2025

## Introduction

DiverseGPT ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

## Information We Collect

### Authentication Information
When you sign up and log in to our service, we collect:
- Email address (for Magic Link authentication)
- Discord profile information (when using Discord authentication), including:
  - Discord username
  - Discord ID
  - Discord avatar
  - Email address associated with your Discord account
- Session tokens for maintaining your login state

### User Content and Interaction Data
To provide our service, we store:
- Chat history and messages
- Custom AI persona configurations
- User preferences and settings

### Payment Information
When you subscribe to our service, we collect:
- Stripe customer information
- Subscription status
- Subscription ID
This information is used solely for managing your subscription and processing payments.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Authenticate your account and maintain login sessions
- Provide and personalize our chat service
- Store and retrieve your chat history
- Save your custom AI persona configurations
- Improve our service and user experience
- Ensure secure access to your account
- Communicate important updates about our service

## Data Storage and Security

- All user data is stored securely in our database
- We use industry-standard encryption for data protection
- Access to user data is strictly limited to authorized personnel
- We regularly update our security measures
- Chat histories and persona configurations are associated with your account and protected

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your personal information with third parties except when:
- Required by law
- Necessary for authentication services (Auth.js, Discord)
- Essential for providing our core services
- You explicitly request it

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

## Cookies and Local Storage

We use:
- Essential cookies for authentication and session management
- Local storage for maintaining user preferences and settings
- Session storage for temporary data

We use these technologies strictly for:
- Maintaining your login session
- Storing user preferences
- Basic application functionality
- Improving user experience

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal information
- Delete your account and all associated data including:
  - Authentication information
  - Chat histories
  - Custom AI persona configurations
  - User preferences
- Request information about your stored data
- Update your account information
- Export your data
- Object to certain data processing

## Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website.

## Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you maintain an active account. This includes:
- Authentication information
- Chat histories
- Custom AI persona configurations
- User preferences and settings

Upon account deletion, all associated data is permanently removed from our systems. Some information might be retained in encrypted backups for a limited time for technical reasons, but will be automatically purged upon account deletion.
